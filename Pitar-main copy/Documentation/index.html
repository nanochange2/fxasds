Template Installation

  1. FTP Upload:
    • Open up your FTP manager and connect to your hosting
    • Browse to required directory (Normally public_html)
    • Upload the files inside Pitar folder.

Pages

    • index.html is for Creative agency Homepage.
    • index-2.html is for Freelancer Homepage.
    • index-3.html is for agency Homepage.
